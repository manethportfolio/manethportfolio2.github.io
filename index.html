Data Visualization projects

Tableau and Power BI, alongside Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), form a powerful trio in the realm of data analysis and business intelligence. These data visualization tools facilitate the creation of interactive dashboards and reports, allowing data professionals to uncover patterns and anomalies during the EDA process. EDA serves as the foundation, enabling data cleaning, transformation, and visualization to reveal critical insights. Tableau's intuitive interface and real-time connectivity, as well as Power BI's robust data handling and integration with Microsoft products, make them indispensable in translating complex data into actionable insights for more informed decision-making and enhanced business intelligence.
